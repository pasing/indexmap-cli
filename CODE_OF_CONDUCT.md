# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We expect all participants to act with respect, professionalism, and empathy.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being considerate and respectful in communication.
- Focusing on the technical issue, not the person.
- Giving and receiving feedback constructively.
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences.
- Taking responsibility and apologizing when mistakes happen.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of behavior that will not be tolerated include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or demeaning comments.
- Personal attacks, insults, or inflammatory language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or contributions.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered abusive or hostile.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

If a participant engages in unacceptable behavior, maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it to the project maintainers directly through the repository's contact channels or by opening a private security or conduct report if available.

Reports will be handled as discreetly and respectfully as possible.
